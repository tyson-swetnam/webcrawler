## Executive Summary

This document provides the complete, verified list of all **187 universities** designated as **R1: Research 1 - Very High Spending and Doctorate Production** in the 2025 Carnegie Classification of Institutions of Higher Education.

### Key Changes in 2025

- **187 total R1 institutions** (up from 146 in 2021)
- **28% increase** in R1-designated universities
- **42 new institutions** gained R1 status in 2025
- **New transparent criteria**: $50M minimum research spending + 70 doctoral degrees annually

### R1 Classification Criteria (2025)

To qualify as R1, institutions must meet both requirements:

1. **Research Spending**: Minimum $50 million in annual R&D expenditures
2. **Doctorate Production**: Minimum 70 research doctorates awarded annually
3. **Measurement Period**: 3-year average (FY 2021-2023) or single-year (FY 2023) data
